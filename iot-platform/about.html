    1.  Smart Trakker for Bike Location


    This is a GPS-enabled device that can be attached to your bike, usually in a discreet location such as under the seat, inside the frame, or in the handlebars. The smart tracker continuously monitors the bike's location in real-time, allowing you to track it through a smartphone app. If your bike is stolen, you can instantly see where it is and provide the authorities with the location to recover it.Key Features:Real-Time Tracking: Constantly updates the bike's location, viewable on your smartphone.Geofencing: You can set a virtual boundary, and if the bike moves outside this area, you'll receive an alert.Battery Life: Many trackers have long-lasting batteries, some even equipped with solar charging.Stealthy Design: Often designed to be difficult for thieves to notice or remove.

    • Real-Time Tracking: Constantly updates the bike's location, viewable on your smartphone.
    •  Geofencing: You can set a virtual boundary, and if the bike moves outside this area, you'll receive an alert
    • Battery Life: Many trackers have long-lasting batteries, some even equipped with solar charging
    •  Stealthy Design: Often designed to be difficult for thieves to notice or remove.

    2.  Smart Ring for Bike Lock Security:


    This smart ring acts as both a locking mechanism and a security alert system. It can either be a separate device or integrated with a smart bike lock. When your bike is locked with this system, the smart ring monitors for any tampering or forced entry attempts. If someone tries to steal your bike, the ring detects the movement or break-in attempt and sends a notification directly to your phone.

    • Proximity Unlocking: Some rings can unlock the bike automatically when you are nearby, making it convenient.
    • Smartphone Notifications: Sends instant alerts to your phone if suspicious activity is detected.
    • Two-Factor Authentication: Adds an extra layer of security by requiring both the ring and a smartphone app to unlock the bike.
    • Tamper Detection: Alerts you instantly if someone tries to tamper with the lock.
